About Me

Hi, I'm Kevin I am an electronic engineering student currently living in sunny South Africa. I enjoy good coffee and love to make music. I write about music, tech, and my other diy/electronics projects.

My online presence has been quite ephemeral the last couple years, but in recent months I have taken to cultivating my own website and trying to make my use of the internet more productive. My goal is to tip the scale and have my time spent creating be more than my time spent consuming and to overall become a more prolific human being.

I tend towards being somewhat of a generalist in my endevours and pride myself on learning and having a broad knowledge.

I also enjoy experimenting and making new and interesting sounds through the use of synthesizers and digital audio workstations. I fancy myself to be a pretty decent cook and like to make food for my family. "Retro" and alternative computing fascinates me and I enjoy learning about different ways that computers have been used historically and how their purpose has changed in everyday life and exploring alternative ways their roles can be filled.
