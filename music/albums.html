albums i like

Albums (mostly from bandcamp) that I enjoyed. I am gradually adding comments to them as they feature in my "Artist Recommendations" on my Bandcamp profile.

  • stray fantasie - mint julep
  • holds - jogginghouse
  • bathtub bumps - badsnacks
  • don't ask - nitemoves
  • fornax void 90s ambient
  • louie zong - jazz
  • Japanese Breakfast - Jubilee
  • Sevish - Bubble
  • ipaghost - weeklybeats
  • Sevish - Harmony Tracker accessible xenharmonic music that plays with your harmonic expectations and introduces microtonal flavours really organically. who said microtonal music has to be weird!
  • Brian Green - Impressions for Headphones
  • Mid Air Thief - Crumbling Beautiful blend of acoustic and electronic elements with soft airy vocals and nice harmony and melody. I can't wait to see what more comes and how the mid air thief mystery unfolds.
  • Garoad - VA-11-HALL-A Second Round
  • Jonathan Ogden - Twenty Four It's a beautifully crafted intimate album, that's just as hifi as it is lofi
  • MUNYA wonderfully atmospheric dream pop with an excellent blend of French and English lyrics that only help to further the atmosphere (the Smashing Pumpkin cover is great too!)
